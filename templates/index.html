Welcome to the Movie Analytics Web App

This web application provides tools to analyze movie data using visualizations and analytics. Built with Neo4j and Apache Spark, the app allows users to explore trends, relationships, and statistics within the movie industry.

Visualize Graph

View a graph of movie industry connections, including actors, directors, and their collaborations.

Average Ratings for 10-Minute Intervals

Analyze how movie ratings vary for films grouped by 10-minute runtime intervals.

Average Ratings by Genre and Decade

Explore trends in average ratings for different genres over each decade and the top-rated movies of each genre.

Most Popular Genres for Actors and Directors

Find the genres that actors and directors are most associated with, based on their movie ratings.

Top Genres by Decade

See the highest-rated genres for each decade and the standout movies within them.

Average Ratings for Actors and Directors

Search for an actor or director and get their average movie ratings.

Individual Statistics

Get detailed stats for a specific actor or director, including their rating trends over time.

Predict Hypothetical Movie Score

Input movie details to predict its potential average score.

Most Collaborated People

Discover pairs of people who frequently collaborate on movies and the films they worked on together.
